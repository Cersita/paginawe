PARRAFO - AIR POLLUTION

Air pollution refers to the presence of harmful substances in the atmosphere, including gases, particles, and biological matter. It is caused by human activities such as industrial emissions, vehicle exhaust, and deforestation, as well as natural events like volcanic eruptions and wildfires.

The consequences of air pollution are significant, impacting human health with respiratory and heart diseases and harming the environment through effects like acid rain and global warming. Solutions to reduce air pollution include lowering emissions, adopting renewable energy sources, and enforcing stricter environmental regulations to protect both people and the planet.
